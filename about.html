About Me

I like building things that solve real problems.

I’m a software engineer based in Philadelphia, focused on creating tools that make hard problems easier.

Whether it’s a product, a system, or a well-placed script, I’m motivated by the challenge of moving the needle. I’ve founded and scaled startups, raised millions in funding, shipped open-source tools, and helped organizations solve technical problems through thoughtful, practical solutions.

I love learning, especially at the edges of my knowledge, and I’m always looking for ways to sharpen my skills. My work spans developer tools, system design, LLMs, and human-computer interaction.

Right now, I’m pursuing my master’s in computer science with a focus on computer security. I also spend a lot of time working on side projects — you can check out some of them here.

When I’m not at my desk, I’m probably reading about something obscure, organizing my spaces (physical or digital), or thinking about how things could be better designed.
